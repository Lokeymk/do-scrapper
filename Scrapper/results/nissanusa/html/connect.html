STAY CONNECTED IN AND WITH YOUR NISSAN

NissanConnect® combines navigation, security, entertainment and ways to stay in touch with a dash mounted system designed to always keep you connected to your Nissan. Want to stream your favorite music wirelessly, remotely start your car with your smartphone or unlock your doors using just your voice with Amazon Alexa or your Google Assistant? [*] NissanConnect® allows you to do this and so much more. [*]
